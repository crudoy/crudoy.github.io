Meet Our Attorneys

Laura Maverick

Founding Partner

Laura Maverick grew up in London, England but moved to the United States with her family in 1995. Upon arrival, Laura put herself through Law School, focusing in Real Property Law as well as civil litigation. Since then, Laura has spent the majority of her time working for non-profit real estate companies that specialize in Section 8 and low income housing.

In 2005, she left the non-profit world and began working at the firm Smith, Smith, and Jones, where she quickly rose from the level of associate counsel to senior counsel. Most of her work as senior counsel focused on neighbor disputes and litigation, though her goal is always mediation when possible.

In 2023, Laura co-founded her law firm, Milo & Maverick A PLC with Kayla Milo, to strive for more equitable housing laws and opportunities among people with low-incomes and minorities. Laura is inovled with the Century City Bar Association, the LGBTQ Bar Association, and the San Fernando Valley Bar Association.

When she isn't working, she is an avid, award-winniing wildlife photographer, traveling across the globe to show as many people as she can the beauty on Earth.

Kayla Milo

Founding Partner

Kayla Milo graduated law school, Summa Cum Laudefrom Columbia Law School in 2013. Since then, Kayla has clerked for Sharon A.M. Aarons, a New York Supreme Court Justice, in 2015 and Elana Kagan, US Supreme Court Justice, in 2019. In 2021, Kayla moved to California to start her own firm in real estate.

In 2023, Kayal co-founded her firm, Milo & Maverick, focusing on real estate disputes to help others acheive their dreams. Her practice handles all matters related to purchase and sale contracts and lot line adjustments. She has also been working with non-profits, such as Legal Aid Foundation of Los Angeles providing pro-bono or discounted legal services to expand access to an attorney to the communities that need it the most. A seasoned attorney, Kayla has been voted "Top Real Estate Attorney" by Super Lawyers Magazine in 2022 and 2023.

In her free time, Kayla enjoys traveling the world, finding new restaurants, and hiking with her dog, Bunny.

Joseph Mia

Partner

Attorney Joseph Mia is California born and raised, growing up in the beach community of Pacific Palisades. From childhood, he knew he wanted to follow in his father's footsteps and become an attorney. Joseph graduated law school from Southwestern University in 2014 and started his own real estate practice shortly after.

Joseph has frequently been called by other attorneys to be a home inspection expert witness, making him an asset to the Maverick & Milo team. He is a guest lecturer at the Los Angeles Attorney Incubator Consortium (LAIC), seeking to provide mentorship and support to new attorneys.

In his free time, Joseph is a marathon runner, competing in half-marathons and marathons throughout the year. Also, he is an avid backpacker and is always planning his next adventure.

Kara Casper

Associate Attorney

Kara Casper began her adult life as an early childhood teacher up in the Bay Area. Upon realizing a number of student's families were involved in legal battles with their homes, Kara had a desire to better understand California real estate law.

A few years later, Kara found her self applying to law school up and down the California coast. Graduating from Pepperdine in 2012, she made her stay in Southern California perminant.

After opening her law practice, Kara's interests narrowed further into neighbor disputes and landlord-tenant cases to help make family lives better in a more direct way.

In her spare time, Kara volunteers at her local YMCA and started an after school program to bring enrichment to kids when they're out of school. She has a dog named Chloe with whom she loves to take on roadtrips back home.

Steve Koda

Associate Attorney

As the son of a firefighter and librarian, Steve always knew he was destined for a life as a public servant, even if that wasn't his first choice. After graduating law school, Steve began his practice as part of the general counsel's office for a health insurance giant. After a few years of that, Steve left that practice and joined Maverick & Milo as their purchasing contracts specialist.

Steve is also a seasoned mediator, helping opposing parties find a solution without having to incur court costs. He also volunteers at non-profits to extend his legal services to those who may not have access to quality representation.

After hours, Steve enjoys spending time coaching his son's little league games and being his daughter's chauffer to the mall.

Reggie Escomo

Associate Attorney

Though he'll always be a Bears fan, Reggie has made a home for himself in Los Angeles, CA. As a first generation American, Reggie found he had two choices for a career path, a doctor or an attorney. After working his way though Law School, Reggie found himself in Sacramento, clerking for a judge on the California Supreme Court.

After a few years, Reggie moved down to Los Angeles to join Maverick & Milo and finally get the California weather he was always promised. As a real estate attorney, Reggie's passion is litigation. Reggie enjoys helping his clients stand up for their legal rights and being a voice for their concerns.

In his free time, Reggie can be found on the pickleball court with his husband, seeking out new restuarants, and back in Chicago (probably at a Bear's game).
